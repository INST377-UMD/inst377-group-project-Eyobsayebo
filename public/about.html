Project Overview

Welcome to the Stock Notification System! This project aims to provide users with real-time information about stock prices, allowing you to stay informed about your favorite stocks.

Information Problem

Our information problem revolves around the need for a user-friendly platform that allows individuals to easily track stock prices, apply filters, and explore popular stocks.

How to Use

To get started, use the search bar to look up specific stocks. You can apply filters based on price and volume to narrow down your results. Additionally, explore popular stocks for quick access to market leaders.
